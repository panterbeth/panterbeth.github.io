Target Audience

Age: 12-100 years old
Income:$45,000-$300,000
Education:High School and above.
Experience:Being outside and looking up weather forecasts.
Family Status:Single, Married, and Parents.

Personas

Fictional Name: Margaret White
Employment: Stay at Home Mother
Demographics: 34 years old. Married. Has four children. Has a Bachelor Degree in Art History.
Goals and Tasks: Checks the weather to ensure her children are well dressed for school and play outside.
Environment: Has a MacBook but uses her phone for most browsing and social media.

Fictional Name: Justin Brown
Employment: Engineer at Boeing
Demographics: 53 years old. Married. Father of 5, none living at home. Has a Masters in Engineering.
Goals and Tasks: Travels with his wife often and likes to plan trips around the weather.
Environment: Prefers to use his desktop Apple computer to have the larger screen when looking at multiple dates and locations around the world.

Scenarios

What will the weather be like in Berlin next week?
Will I be able to soak up some sun at the lake tomorrow?
Should I bundle the kids up or is a light jacket sufficient for the park?
Are we in the path of the hurricane?
Is it supposed to snow this month?
