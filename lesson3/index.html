Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform people about the local weather in their area and in areas they are traveling to. It serves to help them plan their day and activities. Are they going for a hike, swim, bike ride on this beautiful sunny day? Or are the better off staying in with the high chance of rain? It also serves as a place to be informed about coming storms and how to be prepared and to find information about emergency situations such as blackouts and where to go in a weather crisis such as flooding or lightening storm. People will be able to view: hourly, daily, 10day, and monthly forecasts.
