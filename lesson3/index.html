Rays of Sun

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform people about the local weather in their area. It serves to help them plan their day and activities. Are they going for a hike, swim, bike ride on this beautiful sunny day? Or are the better off staying in with the high chance of rain? This website also has a section for community events that are updated based on weather conditions. It also serves as a place to be informed about coming storms and how to be prepared and to find information about emergency situations such as blackouts and where to go in a weather crisis such as flooding or lightening storm. Are you hosting a public event that you want others to know about? Everyone who checks the weather will see your event posted here.
