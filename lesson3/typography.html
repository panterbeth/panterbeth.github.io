Typography

The fonts I have chosen are Fraunces and Overpass Mono. These seemed to provide some contrast with eachother which I think will bring some dynamic to the website. I chose the Fraunces font because it is a little edgy and fun but still easy to read. I will use this font for the headings, as well as the footer on the website. I chose the Overpass Mono font because it is straight forward and easy to read. I will use this font for the paragraphs on the website.
