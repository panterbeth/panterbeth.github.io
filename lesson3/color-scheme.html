The header and headlines will be in the primary color with accent 1 as the text. Normal paragraphs will have white background with secondary text. Colored paragraphs will have accent 2 with text in secondary. The navigation will be secondary with white text. I chose these colors because they match the logo I chose. I thought having the repetition of color would help the site flow.
