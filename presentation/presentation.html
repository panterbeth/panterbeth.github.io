Find a Hike

There are many local hikes in our area and many of them are kid friendly. We have done the work of bringing those all together with explanations of each to help you plan your daytrips. We also have the option to leave reviews of the hikes so that you are able to help other parents know if it is a right fit for them. When you are planning a hike with little ones it is good to know what your time on the trail will be in order to plan snacks, naps, or how long until those littlest of feet get tired. Also, in the information for each hike we have included tips for gear and footwear. Many hikes simply need a baby carrier and tennis shoes since you may be going at the pace of some short legs. Other hikes might be best suited for a day pack, or a child pack to carry those little ones in. So, make sure you are well prepared in order to ensure everyone has a pleasant experience.

Bridalveil Falls:
Hike is 4 miles roundtrip and has a gorgeous waterfall. Kids standing beneath will love to feel the cool water spray on their face. Good for 4+ walkers or baby in carrier.

Granite Falls:
Trail is .7 miles and very easy for even the youngest hiker. Beautiful falls along the granite.

Old Robe Canyon:
Very narrow 2.4 mile trail along old railroad tracks. Good for stable older walkers 4+.
