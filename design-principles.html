Visual Heirarchy

Netflix

Netflix

The main Netflix website demonstrates visual hierarchy with the text on the page. The main focus is unlimited entertainment. The next important is that you can watch anywhere and are you ready to watch now. The email box and get started buttone are roughly the same size as unlimited and therefore equally important.

White Space

Google

Google

Google has a nice clean design. There is a lot of white space which focuses the eye on the Google name and the search bar.

Hicks Law

Zillow

Zillow

The Zillow website demonstrates Hicks Law in that there are only two options on the main page, to enter the location of where you want to search or to sign in.
