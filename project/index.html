Kindness Korner

Every week there are many kind hearted souls in our community that watch out for and provide kind acts of service. This space is reserved to honor them and their good deeds. This week Natalie L. organized a group of friends to report to one another on kind things they had seen others do. She promised that she would bake fresh cookies to the winner of her very own kindness drawing. Each friend enters their name once for each time they either do a kind thing or report on something they witness others doing for them or for others. Natalie is a great example that we can recognize those who are working to lift up others. Thanks Natalie for encouraging us to be better and look for the good.
