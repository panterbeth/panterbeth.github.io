Delicious Geysers!

Article by: Bethany Panter

Visitors to Soda Springs were amazed this week at all the preparations and thought that had gone into the Centennial Celebration of our little town. Many were visiting for the first time and had never before seen our amazing natural carbonated springs. They delighted in the concept and opportunity to mix in their chosen flavorings with the carbonated water to create unique and delectable variations of soda. Visitors were also able to see the wonderful natural geyser we all fondly call Old Soda Fountain. This geyser has long been confused with Old Faithful in Yellowstone but the major difference is Old Soda Fountain’s explosive discharge consists of root beer and not pressurized water. One of the children upon beholding the geyser for the first time exclaimed, “this is the best place ever! Mom can we move here?” Indeed folks, we truly live in the best town on the planet. Go out and enjoy it!
