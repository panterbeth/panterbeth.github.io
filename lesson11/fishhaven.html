Mermaids or Hallucinations?

Article by: Bethany Panter

Early this week a group of fishermen came in from a day of fishing dazed and rambling on about mermaids. These fishermen were out on the lake early in the morning and spotted what they thought was a mermaid in the waters. As they sailed closer the creature disappeared into the depths. They sat searching the water and a school of fish swam past, they attempted to drop their nets to catch this large bounty which would sell well in town. Upon dropping their nets, they saw the mermaid like creature again. This time she looked at them through the depths of water capturing their gaze. As one fisherman stated, “A calm a peace came over me. I no longer had desire to do anything other than stare into her eyes. It was as though she were lookin into me soul.” Fish Haven has long been known as a mystical place and this is not the first-time people have claimed to see mermaids in the water. However, the real question is why these men were attempting to fish when Fish Haven, likes its name is a Haven for fish and no one is to harm them in any way.
