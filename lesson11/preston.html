Earthquakes, Firemen and Shock Waves. Oh my!

Article by: Bethany Panter

This week in Preston we were all shocked, no pun intended by the earthquake we had Saturday night. Preston is not known for having earthquakes and so none of us, my self included were caught off guard. The earthquake was a 7.2 on the Richter scale which is no small quake. There was damage to several homes, and many had bodily injury. However, there was no loss of life thanks to our amazing fire department Preston F.D. These brave men worked around the clock to put out some of the fires that sparked as a result of broken gas lines. They were also able to rescue members of our community caught in upper floors with no way to descend due to broken staircases and shifted houses. These firemen truly, “prest, on” in the face of adversity and held up our town heritage of never giving up even when things seem insurmountable.
